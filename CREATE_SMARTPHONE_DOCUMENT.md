## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific in Main Topic**: 
   - Good: "Smartphones: Evolution, Features, and Impact on Modern Life"
   - Better: "Smartphones: Technology Evolution, Key Features, and Social Impact in 2024"

2. **Refine Strategically**:
   - After generating, use refinement for specific improvements
   - Example: "Add statistics about smartphone usage"

3. **Generate Section by Section**:
   - Don't generate all at once
   - Review each section before moving to the next

4. **Use Feedback**:
   - Like sections you want to keep
   - Add comments for future reference
